Cosmic Nutrition Guide

Your Current Phase

Nourish Your Body

During your menstrual phase, focus on iron-rich foods to replenish what's lost:

  • Dark leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Grass-fed red meat
  • Lentils and beans
  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Vitamin C-rich foods (bell peppers, citrus) to enhance iron absorption

As your energy builds, focus on foods that support estrogen production:

  • Cruciferous vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower)
  • Fermented foods (sauerkraut, kimchi)
  • Omega-3 rich foods (salmon, chia seeds)
  • Fresh sprouts
  • Complex carbs (quinoa, sweet potatoes)

During peak energy days, focus on vibrant, energizing foods:

  • Colorful vegetables (bell peppers, carrots)
  • Lean proteins (chicken, turkey)
  • Raw nuts and seeds
  • Hydrating fruits (watermelon, berries)
  • Digestive enzymes (pineapple, papaya)

As progesterone rises, focus on mood-balancing and grounding foods:

  • Magnesium-rich foods (dark chocolate, almonds)
  • B-vitamin sources (eggs, nutritional yeast)
  • Healthy fats (avocados, olive oil)
  • Root vegetables (beets, carrots)
  • Herbal teas (chamomile, raspberry leaf)

Cosmic Cautions

During your period, avoid these inflammation triggers:

  • Processed sugar
  • Dairy products
  • Caffeine
  • Alcohol
  • Processed meats

While your energy builds, minimize these estrogen disruptors:

  • Soy products (unless fermented)
  • Plastics (store food in glass)
  • Processed vegetable oils
  • Artificial sweeteners
  • Overcooked meats

During your fertile window, avoid these energy drainers:

  • Heavy fried foods
  • Excess salt
  • Artificial additives
  • Raw sprouts (bacteria risk)
  • Undercooked eggs

As PMS may approach, minimize these mood disruptors:

  • Refined carbohydrates
  • Processed snacks
  • Excessive dairy
  • Energy drinks
  • Artificial food dyes
